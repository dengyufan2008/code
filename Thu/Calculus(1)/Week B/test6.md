解方程.

Prob 1. $2xe^{-x^2} \sin y \text{d}x - e^{-x^2} \cos y \text{d}y = 0$.

即 $\frac{\text{d}y}{\text{d}x}=2x \tan y$, $\frac{1}{\tan y} \text{d}y = 2x \text{d}x$, $\ln \sin y = x^2+C$, $y = \arcsin (Ce^{x^2})$.

Prob 2. $(ye^{xy}+\cos x) \text{d}x+xe^{xy}\text{d}y=0$.

即 $\text{d}e^{xy} = -\cos x \text{d}x$, 即 $e^{xy}=-\sin x+C$, 即 $y=\frac{\ln(C-\sin x)}{x}$.

Prob 3. $[x+(x^2+y^2)x^3]\text{d}x+y\text{d}y=0$.

即 $\frac{1}{(x^2+y^2)}\text{d}(x^2+y^2)=-2x^3\text{d}x$, 即 $\ln(x^2+y^2)=-\frac{1}{2}x^4+C$, 即 $\ln(x^2+y^2)+\frac{1}{2}x^4=C$.

Prob 4. $(x-y)\text{d}x+(x+y)\text{d}y=0$.

设 $x=r\cos \theta$, $y=r\sin \theta$, 则

$$r(\cos \theta-\sin \theta) (\cos \theta \text{d}r-r\sin \theta\text{d}\theta)+r(\cos \theta+\sin \theta)(\sin \theta \text{d}r + r \cos \text{d}\theta) = 0.$$

即 $\frac{1}{r}\text{d}r = -\text{d}\theta$, 即 $r = Ce^{-\theta}$.

Prob 5. $2xy\text{d}x+(y^2-3x^2)\text{d}y=0$.

即 $\frac{2x}{y^3} \text{d}x + (\frac{1}{y^2}-\frac{3x^2}{y^4}) \text{d}y=0$, 即 $\text{d}\frac{x^2}{y^3} = -y^2\text{d}y$, 即 $\frac{x^2}{y^3}=-\frac{1}{3}y^3+C$, 即 $y^6-Cy^3+3x^2=0$.
