Prob 1. 求双扭线 $r^2=2a^2\cos 2\theta$ 的弧长, 面积.

我们有 $r=\sqrt 2 a \sqrt{\cos 2 \theta}$, $r'=-\sqrt 2 a \frac{\sin 2 \theta}{\sqrt{\cos 2 \theta}}$.

故弧长为 $2\sqrt 2 a\int_{-\frac{\pi}{4}}^{\frac{\pi}{4}} \sqrt{\cos 2 \theta + \frac{\sin^2 2 \theta}{\cos 2 \theta}} \text{d}\theta = \sqrt 2 a \int_{-\frac{\pi}{2}}^{\frac{\pi}{2}} \cos^{-\frac{1}{2}} \theta \text{d}\theta$.

面积为 $a^2 \int_{-\frac{\pi}{4}}^{\frac{\pi}{4}} \cos 2\theta \text{d}2\theta=2a^2$.
